AI Employee — Trial & Services Agreement

Between Da Birthday Club Limited (NZBN 9429053309198), trading as the AI-employee service ("we", "us"), and the Client named above ("you").

1. What this is. We provide an AI employee — software in a natural human voice that, in your business's name and on your own phone line, handles work such as inbound/outbound calls, appointment setting, follow-ups, customer service and admin. This is a service from a company; it is not employment.

2. Who provides what. We provide the AI employee, setup, human oversight and improvements. You provide a phone line/number (and pay your own call minutes), access to your CRM/system, and your lead list and hours. It runs on your number and in your systems, so it's your caller ID, your brand, and your data stays yours.

3. How we start. (a) A free demo. (b) A 3-day paid trial at the agreed price — results-based, full refund if it doesn't deliver. (c) Optional ongoing at the agreed rate, cancel any time, no notice period.

4. Your data. You are the controller of your data; we only process it to do your work, under the Privacy Act 2020 — confidential, used only for the agreed work, never sold or shared, and deleted / access removed on request.

5. Human oversight. A real person (Humphrey) oversees the AI and steps in if anything's off. You can pause or stop it instantly.

6. We carry the risk on our part. We are responsible for the AI's conduct; if we breach this agreement or are negligent and cause you direct loss, we indemnify you for that direct loss up to the fees you've paid us. You are responsible for your own line, systems and lead data and that you have the right to contact your leads.

7. Compliance. We comply with the Privacy Act 2020, the Fair Trading Act 1986, and NZ telemarketing/calling and electronic-message rules, and disclose that calls are AI-assisted where required.

8. Fees. We invoice the AI service as Da Birthday Club Limited; your telephony/calling costs are on your own account and not marked up. GST added only if/when we are GST-registered.

9. Confidentiality & IP. Both sides keep each other's confidential information private. Our AI system and methods stay ours; your data and results are yours.

10. Liability. Neither side is liable for indirect or consequential loss; our total liability is capped at the fees paid in the prior 3 months. Nothing limits liability that can't legally be limited.

11. Ending it. Either side can end this with notice; the trial guarantee still applies.

12. Law. Governed by New Zealand law; NZ courts have jurisdiction.
